About ASK

Arcada Student Union

ASK is the official student union of Arcada University of Applied Sciences. Founded in 1997, ASK represents student interests, supports well-being, and builds a strong community through events, tutoring, and advocacy.

  • Representation & advocacy
  • Events
  • Tutoring & study support
  • Well-being & anti-harassment support
